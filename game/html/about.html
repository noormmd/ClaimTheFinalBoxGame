This game was created for the second year of the developer's BSc (Hons) in Computer Science. The idea behind the game was greatly inspired by the paper game dots and boxes. After coming across the paper version a year ago, the developer decided to make it a reality in online form.
It is an abstract problem solving stategy game in which the player must claim boxes in grids of varying sizes.
This offers complexity and a great challenge to people who love problem solving.

The developer is a second year Computer Science student at Middlesex University who is building her proficiency in HTML, CSS and JS with this project.
